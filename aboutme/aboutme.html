Hey, I’m Bryan, a young, new web developer. I started my coding journey in January of 2023 via Codecademy, using their “Front-End Engineer” course. I finished it in May of 2023, and have been working on bettering my skills since then.

I was born in Paraguay, but grew up between Canada and Spain due to my parents’ work. I graduated from an American high school in Madrid (Evangelical Christian Academy) in 2021, and then took a year to work in the welding/fabrication industry in Canada. I moved back to Spain after that, and while having a part-time job as a barista at Brilla Café in Madrid, started my journey in coding.

I can’t wait to better my skills and join a work force that will push me to my limits. Thank you for your time, and have a good day.
